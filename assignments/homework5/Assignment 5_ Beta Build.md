# **Assignment 5: Beta Build & Advanced Feature Integration**

**Prerequisite:** Completion of Assignment 4 (Production Plan & Alpha Build).

## **1\. Overview**

In Assignment 4, you established your "Alpha Build" (MVP 2.0), ensuring that your core technical mechanic functioned within a complete software loop (Start, Active, End).

Assignment 5 is about **incremental expansion and visual cohesion**. We are *not* enforcing a feature freeze yet. Instead, the goal of this assignment is to fully integrate your **secondary technical pillars** into the experience. By the end of this phase, your project should transition from a basic prototype into a visually cohesive Beta build, even if some minor mechanics are still being actively developed.

## **1.5 Submission Requirements**

**Added by TA:** Please note that you do not need to beautify your report using LLMs. Reports should be clear, concise and comprehensively reflect your effort on the implementation. It should perform as a tool to help us understand your idea and code.

**Report Format:** Markdown files.

## **2\. Phase 1: Secondary Pillar Integration**

Your primary development focus for this sprint is expanding your vertical slice.

* **Integration:** If your MVP focused heavily on your first pillar (e.g., a custom fluid physics simulation), you must now fully implement and connect your second pillar (e.g., procedural generation of the terrain around the fluid, or advanced lighting/shaders).  
* **Interactivity:** The secondary features must interact meaningfully with your core MVP. They should not feel like separate tech demos running in the same window.  
* **Visual Cohesion:** Begin bridging the gap between "programmer art" and your final aesthetic. The interactive experience should start looking and feeling like a unified product.

## **3\. Phase 2: The Beta Build**

Your submitted software for this assignment is considered your **Beta Build**.

* **Stability:** The build must run smoothly on standard input without crashing.  
* **Progression:** The game loop established in Assignment 4 must remain intact and must now incorporate the new secondary pillar features seamlessly.  
* **Performance Check:** Ensure your new additions (e.g., complex shaders, increased entity counts) are not breaking the target frame rate defined in your Assignment 2 Technical Specifications.
